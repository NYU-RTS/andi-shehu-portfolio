DataBarOnTheRun

An after-hours NYC gathering format for people working across data, analytics, AI, startups, and business.

DataBarOnTheRun was an NYC community initiative built and run to bring together data practitioners, business leaders, founders, and the broader AI community in a format that felt more human than a standard meetup.

The events mixed informal social energy with real technical and professional exchange: roundtable conversations, small-group discussion, hosted moments, and an intentionally approachable environment for people working across data, analytics, AI, and business.

New York City Community-built Small-group format Data and AI network

The format

A quieter, more conversational room

Rather than relying on a lecture-heavy event structure, DataBarOnTheRun used an intimate, discussion-friendly format to help people actually meet, talk, and exchange ideas across technical and business lines. That made it easier to connect practitioners with operators, collaborators, and decision-makers in a way that felt direct and memorable.

The work behind the scenes included organizing the format, hosting the room, shaping the flow of the event, and creating a space where conversation could move naturally between community, applied practice, and broader questions around data and AI.

  • Convened practitioners, founders, and business-facing attendees in NYC.
  • Used a small-group format that encouraged real discussion instead of passive attendance.
  • Blended social atmosphere with professional exchange in a way that felt memorable and personal.
