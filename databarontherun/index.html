DataBarOnTheRun

A small New York event series I ran for people working across data, analytics, startups, and business.

I started DataBarOnTheRun because I wanted something smaller and less formal than the usual meetup. The gatherings brought together people from data, analytics, AI, startups, and business who would not usually end up in the same room.

Some nights were more social and some were closer to roundtables. The idea stayed the same: keep the group small, make it easy for people to talk, and give the evening just enough structure without turning it into a panel or talk.

Format

How the evenings worked

The format stayed small. That made it easier for people to meet, stay in conversation, and get past quick introductions.

I handled the organizing, outreach, hosting, and overall flow. What I liked most was seeing technical people, founders, and people from the business side actually spend time talking with one another.

  • Brought together people from data, AI, startups, and business in New York.
  • Kept the gatherings small enough for actual discussion.
